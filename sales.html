The 5 widgets, and what each one does for the sale

W1
Their Situation Shows the customer you've listened. They read their own reality back to them — that alone sets you apart from every other MSP.
W2
Why Now Creates urgency without pressure. Connects the cost of inaction to something already happening in their world.
W3
What We Do Positions you as the expert. Not a feature list — a clear explanation of the outcome they're buying.
W4
Why Us Answers the question every buyer is silently asking. Differentiates on trust and track record, not price.
W5
Next Steps Removes the last objection before they sign. Makes it easy to say yes — low friction, clear action.

People buy emotionally and justify logically. The widgets lead with empathy, then evidence, then confidence — in that order, always.
